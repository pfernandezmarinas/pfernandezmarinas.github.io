ECORRUPTION

This game was developed as a final project for one of the subjects of the Master's Degree in Computer Graphics, Games and Virtual Reality in the Rey Juan Carlos University with:

  • Jaime Elcarte Fontcuberta
  • Miguel Medina Patón
  • Clara Peñalva Carbonell
  • José María Pizana García
  • Cristian Soler Sierra


    • ECORRUPTION is a management and strategy game in which the player is the leader of an ecologist political party that just won the elections. As the mayor, the player must maintain the electors favor, be loyal to the political party's principles and keep the industries on their side.

    CHARACTERISTICS:

  • Turn-based game.
  • Procedural generated hexagonal map based on Catlike Coding's tutorial.
  • Database created using scriptable objects. That makes easy to add new types of cells, actions and events.
  • Data saved using JSON files.
  • Effects created with shaders, particles systems and postprocessing
  • Developed in Unity using C#.

    • Download and play it from https://pizanagd.itch.io/ecorruption.
